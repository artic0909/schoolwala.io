In this fun and engaging lesson, we'll explore the world of large numbers! You'll learn:

  • How to read and write numbers up to 10 lakhs (1,000,000)

  • How to compare large numbers using greater than and less than symbols

Get ready to become a number ninja! By the end of this video, you'll be able to confidently handle big numbers in your everyday life.
